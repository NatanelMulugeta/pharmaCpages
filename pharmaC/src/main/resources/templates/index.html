PharmaC

PharmaC is dedicated to providing up to date information to all pharmacy users in the city of Addis Ababa. We provide a platform that enables users to search needed medicines and provide the names and locations of pharmacies who have those medicines. Users can create accounts to get notifications of newly opened pharmacies and rate pharmacies. Pharmacists and pharmacy owners are encouraged to create accounts to update their data, evaluate their ratings and help their users save precious time and energy.

Looking for a particular medicine?

PharmaC is developed to help people like you. We save your time and energy by telling you where all the good medicines are stocked up. Click "looking for a particulat medicine?" if you are here to search for a pharmacy that has the medicine you need.
